# How your store works

A short guide to the things you'll do most often. Bookmark this or
print it — it lives in your project folder at
`.bodega/handoff/how-your-store-works.md`.

## Logging in

Your studio is at {studio_url}. To sign in:

1. Open that link (or click the button in any email from us)
2. Type your email
3. We send you a one-time link — click it, you're in

No passwords. No 2FA apps. If you lose a link, request a new one from
the login page.

## Adding a product

From your studio home:

1. Tap **+ New product**
2. Take a photo (or upload one from your phone)
3. Give it a name, a price, a short description
4. Tap **Publish**

That's it. It'll show up on your store within a minute.

## Seeing orders

Your studio home shows today's orders at the top. Tap any order to see:
- What they bought
- Shipping address
- Amount paid

## Shipping an order

From the order detail screen:

1. Tap **Print label** (we'll show you the cheapest shipping option)
2. Print on regular paper, tape it to the package
3. Drop at USPS, UPS, or wherever fits
4. Tap **Mark shipped** so your customer gets a tracking email

## Getting paid

Your money goes straight to the bank account you set up in Stripe.
Default payout schedule is weekly, but you can change it in Stripe's
dashboard.

To see your payouts and earnings:
- In Stripe: dashboard.stripe.com → Payouts
- Quick overview in your studio: **Settings → Money**

## Common questions

**Someone wants to return something.**
Open the order, tap **Refund**. Choose full or partial. Stripe handles
the rest.

**A shipping label didn't print right.**
In the order detail, tap **Regenerate label**. Shippo holds up to 14
days of label history, so you can reprint free.

**My photo is too big / won't upload.**
Photos must be under 10MB. Most phone photos are fine. If not, any
photo editor can shrink it.

**I want to change my shop's colors / fonts / name.**
That's a conversation with {operator_first_name} — the design of your
site lives with them. For store *settings* (shipping, tax, return
policy), those are all in **Settings** in your studio.

## When something goes wrong

Reply to any email from us. Our team sees it within a few hours
(faster on weekdays). We'll help.

If the site is down or checkout is broken, tell us immediately and we'll
investigate — don't wait.
