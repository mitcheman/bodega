# Announcement blurb

Short, iMessage-shaped message the operator can forward. Customize in
review before sending.

## Default (handoff to spouse / close friend)

> Hey — your store is live at {store_url} 🎉
>
> Login link I just sent you is in your email. First thing: add your
> first {product_noun_singular} at {studio_url}. Takes about 2 minutes.
>
> Let me know if anything's weird.

## Alternative (handoff to client)

> Hi {merchant_first_name} — {store_name} is live at {store_url}.
>
> I just emailed you a login link for your studio. That's where you'll
> add products, see orders, and print shipping labels.
>
> I'll be around for the next couple of days if you run into anything.
> After that, the support email in your welcome email is the fastest
> way to get help.

## Casual (solo, for your own records)

> {store_name} is live → {store_url}
> Studio → {studio_url}
